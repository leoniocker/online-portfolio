50 postcards in 4 weeks!

Discovering Singapore.


 

This was a project conducted in April 2016. The mission was to make and send 50 postcards for and of random people in Singapore. The project had several benefits as I got to learn more about Singapore and its citizens, but also learned more about myself.

 

 

What is an act of courage for you? What can you do, that you would normally never dare to do? Can you do 3 Acts of Courage in one day and share your experience?

  • About the Project:

  • This project appeared after doing an intensive coaching training at the company "Executive Coach International". The Programme had an add-on programme called "Making Courage Work". Within this porject we had to give ourselves a project for the following 4 weeks that had to give some meaning to ourselves. I chose to create 50 postcards for 50 strangers in Singapore. That didn't just allow me to play around more with Photoshop and discover more places in Singapore, but I also had to overcome the fear of speaking to strangers on the street and getting their address, which, as a reward, they will receive a self-made postcard, made by me.
